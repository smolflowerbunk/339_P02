Shipping and Returns

We are pleased to offer $10 flat rate standard shipping for all orders shipped to Canadian addresses. Standard shipping is 5-7 days from the moment your order is processed until delivery at your doorstep.

For U.S. based orders, shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

We are pleased to offer shipping to the United States!

For orders to U.S. addresses, please be aware that any duties, taxes, or other fees imposed by customs are the responsibility of the order recipient. Additionally, shipping rates may vary depending on shipping location.

We aim to ship our all orders placed before 11AM PST out on the same day, but during peak holiday seasons this may extend to a 24-48 hour timeframe due to a higher volume of orders being placed and product availability.

Using the order number in your confirmation email, please visit our order tracking page to view your order status.

We take great pride in the satisfaction of our customers, and this includes every step of the experience from browsing our selection to the moment our chocolate is delivered to your hands. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we are unable to accept returns or exchanges for items that are not defective.

If you find that your order is incorrect, damaged, or defective, please call our store at 604-123-4567 or email us at customercare@petitamours.com within 48 hours of delivery so we can appropriately address the situation.

If you have not received an order which is marked as “delivered” under order tracking, please email us at customercare@petitamours.com so we can appropriately address the situation.
